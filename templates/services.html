Personal Training


This program offers workouts tailored to the individual and is designed to reach your workout goals as fast and safe as possible. Our skilled staff of trainers is highly skilled in several areas of physical fitness, keeping the workouts fun and exciting by mixing in a variety of exercises such as kettle bells, resistance training, boxing, etc.



  • Toning and Flexibility:
    Light weight and body weight exercises designed to tone up and strengthen those stubborn muscles in target areas. In addition, we conduct a hands-on stretching session to increase flexibility. This session will leaving you looking and feeling great!

  • Weight Loss and Fat Burn:
    Our Motto is “Sweat is Fat Crying!” We will show you how to burn fat and shed those unwanted pounds in a fun and private workout that is unmatched in this area.

  • Teen Training:
    With young trainers and a fun environment we will get your teen motivated to work hard and get in great shape. Understanding from a young age the importance of working out and learning the correct form, will teach your teen valuable lessons that will be applied for their entire life.

  • Body Building:
    Build ripped muscle mass! Tired of working hard and not getting the results you want? Come to G Fitness and see those skin tearing results you have only dreamed about by pushing to the limit in a program that works.

  • Nutrition:
    Let our expert nutritionist put together a customized meal plan designed to safely and effectively help lose weight and curb your appetite. We can show you how substituting little things can go a long way!
